About Us

Our company is dedicated to making games that are focused on quality and bring immeasurable entertainment to all who play our games. While our compmay isn't as big as the major competitors that are in the video game industry, we will do our best to create games that are on par with those games. We focus on making games of various genres such as 2D and 3D platformers, shooters, and adventure games. Our website here provides more general information about us and our current future plans for our company.

The image above is the founder of our company: Cameron Winters. Cameron was introduced to video games when, at a very young age, he caught a glimpse of his uncle doing something with the TV as he was following his grandmother around her house. He got a closer look and had his first look and experience of a video game. Since that moment, Cameron began to enjoy video games whether that be at home or arcade games. He even began to start coding his own game that would soon become known as Personalities. It would go on to become a major success and would be the game to jumpstart his carreer and create Pixel Rush Games. Cameron's friends would come along and help out later on. From there, the company would expand and become another competitor in the video game industry.

Current News

Last Updated 3/01/24

We have major future plans for the overall advancement of our company. First off we have been working on a few new titles that we hope will bring excitement to everyone who plays them. One of these is a first-person horror game called Faded Memories. After losing your way in the woods while on a camping trip, you come across an abandoned town that lays in ruin from some sort of disaster. As you explore, you will uncover the secrests of the people who once inhabited the town and what exactly happened to them. Be aware though, even though the town seems abandoned, you are not alone! The picture above is an early screenshot of the game and is expected to be released this October.

As for other plans, we are working on various other titles such as the third instalment in the Personalities series where we will be attempting to bring the series into the world of 3D while still keping it's original charm. We have also began development on a new title called Explorer: an open-world game where you can build your own structures and explore various environments ranging from a mountainous forest to a extensive ocean. An early screenshot can be sen below on the left. Finally, we have begun brainstorming for an action-shooter game taking inspiration from Fortnite and Splatoon. We will share more details as we make developments on these projects. We are also working out deals with other companies to create licensed merchandise for some of our IPs. Our plan is to launch with merchandise from the Personalities series with figurines, clothing, and other miscellaneous merchandise. Check this website weekly for any new updates.
